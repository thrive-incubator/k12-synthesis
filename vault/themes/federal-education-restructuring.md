## Current snapshot

The administration's dismantling and outsourcing of the U.S. Department of Education is being
contested and codified at the same time. Ten congressional bills would write the outsourcing of
federal education programs to other agencies into statute — making the restructuring durable
regardless of litigation. On the special-education front, former federal officials publicly oppose
the ED–HHS interagency agreement covering IDEA administration, while a report found ED has scaled
back its monitoring of states' special-education compliance. Net effect: states and districts
inherit growing ambiguity about where federal K12 money and rules will come from.
*(as of 2026-W29)*
