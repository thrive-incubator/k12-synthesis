## Current snapshot

HRSA (part of HHS) is an active federal funder of pediatric health-access programs relevant to
student wellbeing. As of this week its [[pediatric-mental-health-care-access-program]] (PMHCA),
which supports pediatric mental-health care access including tele-consultation models that reach
students, is posted and closing July 10, 2026.
*(as of 2026-W27)*
