## Current snapshot

SAMHSA (part of HHS) is an active federal funder of behavioral and mental-health services relevant
to schools, and its summer NOFO slate is in its final windows: state primary/behavioral-health
integration (SM-26-021) and the Center of Excellence for Protected Health Information Related to
Behavioral Health (TI-26-020) both close **July 27, 2026**, and the three parallel CCBHC
solicitations (SM-26-014/-015/-016) close **August 17, 2026**. The school-specific
[[trauma-informed-services-in-schools]] program (SM-26-006) closed July 16, and the mobile-crisis,
early-diversion, and tribal suicide-prevention NOFOs closed mid-July.
*(as of 2026-W30)*
