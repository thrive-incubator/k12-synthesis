## Current snapshot

SAMHSA (part of HHS) is an active federal funder of behavioral and mental-health services relevant
to schools. As of this week it has multiple grant opportunities open, including the school-specific
[[trauma-informed-services-in-schools]] program (SM-26-006, closes July 16, 2026) and a Behavioral
Health Partnerships for Early Diversion of Adults and Youth program (closes July 15, 2026).
*(as of 2026-W27)*

## Timeline

### 2026-W27 — Trauma-Informed Services in Schools and youth diversion grants open
*Grants.gov · June 16, 2026* · [link](https://www.grants.gov/search-results-detail/SM-26-006)
SAMHSA's Trauma-Informed Services in Schools program (SM-26-006) is posted, opened June 16 and
closing July 16, 2026. SAMHSA also has a Behavioral Health Partnerships for Early Diversion of
Adults and Youth program closing July 15, 2026.
