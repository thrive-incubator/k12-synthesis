## Current snapshot

SAMHSA (part of HHS) is an active federal funder of behavioral and mental-health services relevant
to schools, and its summer NOFO slate is hitting its close dates: the school-specific
[[trauma-informed-services-in-schools]] program (SM-26-006) closes July 16, 2026; mobile crisis
team partnerships and early-diversion NOFOs closed July 15; tribal suicide prevention closes
July 16; state primary/behavioral-health integration closes July 27; and three parallel CCBHC
solicitations run to August 17, 2026.
*(as of 2026-W29)*
