## Current snapshot

CMS (part of HHS) sets the payment rails that school-adjacent behavioral-health billing runs on.
It has proposed the CY 2027 Physician Fee Schedule rule (published July 16, 2026) — the annual
vehicle for Part B payment and coverage policy, including telehealth and behavioral-health payment
updates, whose benchmarks many state Medicaid programs key off.
*(as of 2026-W30)*
