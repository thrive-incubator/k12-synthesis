## Current snapshot

The Pediatric Mental Health Care Access Program (PMHCA) is an open HRSA grant supporting pediatric
mental-health care access, including tele-consultation models that reach students. As of this week
it is posted and closes **July 10, 2026**. Administered by [[hrsa]].
