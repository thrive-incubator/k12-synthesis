## Current snapshot

The Florida Mental Health Assistance Allocation is a state school-mental-health funding vehicle that
districts and charter schools access via an approved Mental Health Assistance Plan. As of this week,
Cape Coral's Oasis charter schools approved their plan to tap the allocation — an example of the
charter sector using the same state school-mental-health stream as districts.
*(as of 2026-W27)*
